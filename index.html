Frequently asked questions
Have a specific question? Call us
Do you come to our home? And where do you park?
Yes we come to your home we will park on the street or in your driveway.
How long does it take to groom?
Depending on size, temperament, and haircut anywhere from 1 hour and 30 min to 2 hours
How long have you been in the business of grooming?
7 years of experience
Can I see the grooming space?
Yes, it's clean and stress free
How is your pricing structured?
Based on the clients needs, simple packages and pampering add-ons
What are the steps to ensure safety?
One to one service. The client is never left alone also, first aid kit.
Do you need water or electricity?
No the vehicle is fully equipped to take care of the clients needs.
Do you do Medicated Baths and Nails only ?
Yes for a service fee.
Why is mobile grooming more than a pet shop ?
You stay in the comfort of your home. We come to you, your dog stays in a familiar environment. You can relax.
What if I need you after hours?
For an additional service fee we can add in a non emergency house call.
Are you certified?
Yes.
All dogs groomed by weight and breed,
